30 years of development has provided homes for more that 40 thousand people

20 thousand apartments completed in 200 projects

CENTRAL GROUP is the largest residential developer in the Czech Republic. During its 30 years in business, it has sold more than 20,000 new apartments, houses and plots in 200 residential projects. Thanks to CENTRAL GROUP, more than 40,000 people now have new homes, which is equivalent to a large district town, such as Mladá Boleslav.

35K apts for 70K people
in preparation in new districts across Prague

CENTRAL GROUP owns more than 1.5 million m² of land and brownfields in Prague for the construction of more than 35,000 apartments. It focuses on transforming large neglected brownfields into pleasant green living spaces that also benefit their surroundings. The company is developing entire new urban districts as well as smaller exclusive residential projects.

CENTRAL GROUP collaborates
with the best architects

CENTRAL GROUP has its own in-house team of 120 architects and designers, making it one of the largest architectural-design firms in the country. For large residential districts, it organizes major architectural competitions and workshops. Thanks to this, it collaborates with the best architects and urban planners not only from the Czech Republic, but also from abroad.

30 years of growth
„from zero“ to the new housing market leader

Founding of CENTRAL GROUP and land sales

In its early development days, the company performed parceling for owners of large land areas and sold individual building plots while also ensuring infrastructure investment for the parcels.

Constructing family houses

The company begins building family houses, initially solitary ones for clients who purchased plots from it, and later entire large projects of detached and terraced houses, and eventually small apartment buildings on the outskirts of Prague.

Constructing apartment buildings

CENTRAL GROUP begins building larger apartment buildings in Prague (the first major construction being an apartment building in Vršovice) and the company also shifts its focus from parcels and family houses to apartment construction while implementing an advanced three-stage quality management system.

Constructing entire residential neighborhoods

The company enters the field of large-scale development of entire residential neighborhoods and builds Nové Zahradní Město (New Garden City) with approximately 1,200 apartments, and with the number of sold and planned apartments, it becomes the leader in the residential market in the Czech Republic.

Constructing prestigious high-rise buildings

CENTRAL GROUP further strengthens its position as a leader in residential development, building many projects (including dominant high-rise buildings Prague Towers and subsequently Garden Towers) and begins to specialize in the construction of entire new urban districts on brownfield sites.

Entry into the luxury apartment segment

CENTRAL GROUP enters the luxury housing market and begins construction of exclusive projects (Residence Park Nikolajka in Prague's Smíchov district and Residence U Muzea in the very heart of the city) and starts collaboration with architect Josef Pleskot.

Constructing the largest projects in Prague

CENTRAL GROUP begins construction of its two largest projects to date - the luxury Rezidence Parková čtvrť in Prague 3 and the new Tesla Hloubětín district in Prague 9. Both quickly become the best-selling residential projects in Prague.

30 years in the market and 35,000 more apartments

The company celebrates 30 years in the market as the long-term largest residential developer in the Czech Republic, having sold 20 thousand properties, and is preparing 35 thousand more apartments in new urban districts throughout Prague. The company is also developing extensive collaboration with architect Eva Jiřičná in two large locations.

CENTRAL GROUP is preparing to expand operations throughout the Czech Republic as part of the new "Affordable rental apartments for cities and municipalities using the DESIGN‑BUILD method" program.
